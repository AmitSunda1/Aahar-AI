The Totals

Calories: Approximately 1,010 kcal

Protein: Around 43g

Carbohydrates: About 128g

Fats: Roughly 37g

Breakdown by Dish

Paneer Curry: This is your primary source of protein and fat, contributing about 12g of protein and 18g of fat.

Dal & Chana Masala: These legumes add a combined 16g of protein and are great for fiber, though they also carry about 38g of carbs.

Jeera Rice & Rotis: These make up the bulk of the energy here. Together, they provide about 72g of carbohydrates and 10g of protein.

Raita & Sides: The curd and salad add a light 5g of protein and some essential micronutrients while keeping the extra calories low.
